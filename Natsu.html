About Natsu

Age: 19

Magic: Fire Dragon Slayer Magic

Guild: Fairy Tail

Description: Natsu is the main protagonist of Fairy Tail. He's a Dragon Slayer wizard who can eat fire to gain power. He's known for his fiery personality and his strong bond with his friends.

Abilities

  • Fire Dragon Slayer Magic
  • Enhanced strength and durability
  • Can eat fire to regain power
  • Motion sickness (can't ride vehicles)
