Dragon Slayer Magic

The most powerful form of magic, taught by dragons themselves.

Fire Dragon Slayer Magic

User: Natsu Dragneel

Description: Allows the user to eat fire to gain power and use fire-based attacks.

Special Abilities: Can eat fire to regain magic power, immune to fire damage.

  • Fire Dragon's Roar
  • Fire Dragon's Iron Fist
  • Fire Dragon's Wing Attack
  • Fire Dragon's Claw

Lightning Dragon Slayer Magic

User: Laxus Dreyar

Description: Allows the user to control lightning and electricity.

Special Abilities: Can eat lightning to regain power, extremely fast attacks.

  • Lightning Dragon's Roar
  • Lightning Dragon's Iron Fist
  • Lightning Dragon's Slayer Secret Art

Demon Slayer Magic

Powerful magic designed specifically to slay demons, often with dark and destructive properties.

Ice Demon Slayer Magic

User: Gray Fullbuster

Description: A powerful form of ice magic that can slay demons. Gray learned this from his father, Silver Fullbuster, who was a demon.

Special Abilities: Can create ice weapons and structures, but with demon-slaying properties. The ice is darker and more destructive than regular ice magic.

  • Ice Demon Slayer: Ice-Make Lance
  • Ice Demon Slayer: Ice-Make Shield
  • Ice Demon Slayer: Ice-Make Floor
  • Ice Demon Slayer: Ice-Make Hammer
  • Ice Demon Slayer: Ice-Make Cannon
  • Ice Demon Slayer: Ice-Make Sword

Note: Gray originally used regular Ice-Make Magic, but later developed Ice Demon Slayer Magic after learning about his father's true nature.

Celestial Spirit Magic

Magic that allows users to summon spirits from the Celestial Spirit World.

Celestial Spirit Magic

User: Lucy Heartfilia

Description: Uses magical keys to summon spirits who fight alongside the mage.

Special Abilities: Each spirit has unique powers and abilities.

  • Aquarius (Water Spirit)
  • Leo (Lion Spirit)
  • Virgo (Maiden Spirit)
  • Scorpio (Scorpion Spirit)
  • Gemini (Twin Spirits)

Requip Magic

Magic that allows instant changing of weapons and armor.

Requip: The Knight

User: Erza Scarlet

Description: Can instantly change armor and weapons for different situations.

Special Abilities: Each armor set provides different powers and abilities.

  • Heaven's Wheel Armor
  • Flame Empress Armor
  • Lightning Empress Armor
  • Black Wing Armor
  • Adamantine Armor

Take Over Magic

Magic that allows users to take on the form and abilities of other beings.

Satan Soul

User: Mirajane Strauss

Description: Allows the user to take on demon forms and abilities.

Special Abilities: Each form has different powers and appearances.

  • Satan Soul: Seilah
  • Satan Soul: Halphas
  • Satan Soul: Sitri
  • Satan Soul: Alegria
