About Gray

Age: 18

Magic: Ice-Make Magic

Guild: Fairy Tail

Description: Gray is a powerful Ice-Make wizard and one of Fairy Tail's strongest members. He's known for his rivalry with Natsu and his habit of unconsciously stripping off his clothes. He's cool-headed in battle but has a hot temper.

Abilities

  • Ice-Make Magic
  • Can create weapons and structures from ice
  • Enhanced physical strength
  • Expert hand-to-hand combat
  • Ice Devil Slayer Magic (later in series)

Notable Ice Creations

  • Ice Blade
  • Ice Shield
  • Ice Cannon
  • Ice Hammer
  • Ice Floor
