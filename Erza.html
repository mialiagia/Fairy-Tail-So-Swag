About Erza

Age: 19

Magic: Requip Magic

Guild: Fairy Tail

Description: Erza is an S-Class Mage and one of Fairy Tail's strongest members. She's known for her Requip magic, which allows her to instantly change armor and weapons. She's strict and disciplined but deeply cares for her guildmates.
