S-Class Guilds

Fairy Tail

Location: Magnolia Town

Master: Makarov Dreyar

Rank: S-Class

Description: The main guild of the series, known for its strong bonds of friendship and family. Despite being considered a "problem guild," its members are incredibly powerful and loyal to each other.

Notable Members

  • Natsu Dragneel (Dragon Slayer)
  • Lucy Heartfilia (Celestial Spirit Mage)
  • Gray Fullbuster (Ice-Make Mage)
  • Erza Scarlet (Requip Mage)
  • Wendy Marvell (Sky Dragon Slayer)
  • Gajeel Redfox (Iron Dragon Slayer)
  • Laxus Dreyar (Lightning Dragon Slayer)
  • Mirajane Strauss (Take Over Mage)

Sabertooth

Location: Crocus

Master: Jiemma

Rank: S-Class

Description: One of the most powerful guilds in Fiore, known for its strict hierarchy and powerful members. They often compete with Fairy Tail.

Notable Members

  • Sting Eucliffe (White Dragon Slayer)
  • Rogue Cheney (Shadow Dragon Slayer)
  • Minerva Orland (Requip Mage)
  • Rufus Lore (Memory-Make Mage)
  • Orga Nanagear (Lightning God Slayer)

A-Class Guilds

Blue Pegasus

Location: Hargeon Town

Master: Bob

Rank: A-Class

Description: Known for its handsome male members and their focus on beauty and charm. They are allies of Fairy Tail.

Notable Members

  • Ichiya Vandalay Kotobuki (Perfume Magic)
  • Hibiki Lates (Archive Magic)
  • Eve Tearm (Snow Magic)
  • Ren Akatsuki (Human Subordination Magic)

Lamia Scale

Location: Oak Town

Master: Ooba Babasaama

Rank: A-Class

Description: A respected guild known for its strong female members and traditional values.

Notable Members

  • Jura Neekis (Earth Magic)
  • Sherry Blendy (Doll Magic)
  • Toby Horhorta (Sand Magic)
  • Yuka Suzuki (Plant Magic)

Mermaid Heel

Location: Hargeon Town

Master: Kagura Mikazuchi

Rank: A-Class

Description: An all-female guild known for their beauty and strength. They specialize in water-based magic.

Notable Members

  • Kagura Mikazuchi (Gravity Magic)
  • Millianna (String Magic)
  • Beth Vanderwood (Plant Magic)
  • Risley Law (Memory-Make Magic)

B-Class Guilds

Phantom Lord

Location: Oak Town (Formerly)

Master: Jose Porla (Former)

Rank: B-Class (Disbanded)

Description: Formerly one of the most powerful guilds, known for its dark magic and ruthless tactics. They were rivals of Fairy Tail until their defeat.

Former Notable Members

  • Gajeel Redfox (Iron Dragon Slayer) - Now in Fairy Tail
  • Juvia Lockser (Water Magic) - Now in Fairy Tail
  • Aria (Air Magic)
  • Totomaru (Fire Magic)
  • Solly (Plant Magic)

Quatro Cerberus

Location: Oak Town

Master: Goldmine

Rank: B-Class

Description: A guild known for its unique members and their unconventional magic styles.

Notable Members

  • Bacchus Groh (Drunken Fist)
  • Rocker (Rock Magic)
  • Warcry (Sound Magic)
  • Nobarly (Noble Magic)

C-Class and Smaller Guilds

Raven Tail

Location: Various
